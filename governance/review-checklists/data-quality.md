# Data Quality Review Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing validation logic, QA guidance, quality documentation, or quality-related implementation artifacts.

- Is the quality scope explicit?
- Is the affected component or environment identified?
- Are Medallion boundaries respected in the quality approach?
- Are data assumptions stated clearly?
- Are validation expectations appropriate for the target environment?
- Does the change preserve lineage, traceability, and reviewability?
- Is the quality approach realistic for enterprise use without becoming over-specified?
